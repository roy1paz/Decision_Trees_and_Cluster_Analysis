About the data¶

This dataset is all about credit card defaulters. Our aim is to propose cluster deviation of the records into clusters using distance between records.

The dataset contains 5 Columns

  • 1) ID - Id of customer
  • 2) Default - Is the person a loan defaulter
  • 3) Student - Is the person a student
  • 4) Balance - Balance in his/her account
  • 5) Income - His/Her income
