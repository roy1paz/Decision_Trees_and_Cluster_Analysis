About the data:¶

this dataset contains COVID-19 information about 225 countries. our aim is to cluster those countries by severity of the pandemic.

features:

  • country - the name of the country, we use it a index.
  • other names - other name of the country
  • ISO 3166-1 alpha-3 CODE - three-letter country codes defined in ISO 3166-1
  • Population - number of people in the country
  • Continent - the continent of the country
  • Total Cases
  • Total Deaths
  • Tot Cases//1M pop - Total Covid 19 cases per million population
  • Tot Deaths/1M pop - Total deaths related to Covid 19 cases per million population
  • Death percentage - Total Death per Total Covid 19 cases reported

from sklearn.cluster import KMeans
import warnings

warnings.filterwarnings("ignore", category=FutureWarning)
k_list = []
vals = range(1, 11)  
for k in vals:
    algorithm = KMeans(n_clusters=k)
    algorithm.fit(data)
    k_list.append(algorithm.inertia_)
plt.plot(range(1, 11) , k_list, marker='o')
plt.xlabel('k')
plt.title('Elbow Method')
plt.show()

kmeans = KMeans(n_clusters=2)
kmeans.fit(data)
plt.scatter(data.iloc[:, 0], data.iloc[:, 1], c=kmeans.labels_, cmap='viridis')
plt.scatter(kmeans.cluster_centers_[:, 0], kmeans.cluster_centers_[:, 1], c='red', marker='x')
plt.title(f'KMeans Clustering (K={2})')
